So you want to be a Plebian

In order to be a plebian, you must know how to farm, bake, build and/or craft. You must also know how to provide for your group and friends. Being a hard worker would also be very necessary since we must fight in order to get food and pay our taxes.

Although we have been shut out of governing positions, we protect each other which helps us be able to pay for necessities.

Our Responsibilities are that we protect some of the basic rights of all Roman citizens nonmatter of their social class.
